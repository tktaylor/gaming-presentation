Short Introduciton

Gaming has been a hobby of mine since I was a very small child. My interest in it has only grown over the years.

I started out with console gaming and have owned a variety of consoles. A few years after that, still in my childhood, I had started playing computer games for the Mac. Later on, I switched to Windows/PC, which is currently my primary source of gaming now.

Styles of Games

  • Board Games
    • Different styles of board games
      • Competitive - any game that has you playing against one or more players
        • Monopoly
        • Risk
        • Settlers of Catan
        • Chess
        • Scrabble
        • Game of Life
      • Cooperative - one or more players team up to complete some objective or goal to "win" the game, essentially playing against the game itself
        • Pandemic
        • Arkham Horror
        • Betrayal at House on the Hill
        • Spirit Island

        I find cooperative board games to be more fun, but there are few mainsteam games in this style.
